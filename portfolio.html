About Me

Hello! My name is Milan Oli, and I am a passionate and motivated Class 12 student from Rajapur-6, Pahadinagar (Kuniya), Bardiya, Nepal. Currently, I am pursuing my studies in the field of Physical Science, but my true passion lies in web development.

Ever since I was introduced to the world of coding, I have been captivated by the endless possibilities that it offers. My journey as a web developer began with learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allowed me to create interactive and visually appealing websites. I enjoy the process of transforming ideas into functional and user-friendly interfaces, and the satisfaction of seeing my work come to life on the web.

While studying Physical Science has provided me with a strong foundation in scientific principles, I have taken it upon myself to dive deeper into web development by continuously learning and expanding my skill set. I have explored frameworks like Bootstrap and libraries like jQuery to enhance my ability to create responsive and dynamic web pages.

Aside from technical skills, I believe that effective communication and collaboration are crucial in delivering successful web projects. I have actively wanted to participated in group projects . I am always eager to learn from others and embrace feedback as a means of personal and professional growth.

In the future, my goal is to secure a job as a web developer where I can apply my skills and contribute to creating innovative digital experiences. I am driven by a desire to continuously evolve as a developer and keep up with the latest trends and technologies in the industry.
